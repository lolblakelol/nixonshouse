The Main Man

Nixon

He's easygoing, cuddly, and will make himself right at home with you. He'll talk your ear off and sleep most of the day — just how he likes it.

🍽
Feeding
  • Dry food is self-serve — he grazes all day. Refill every couple of days. Stored to the right of the washing machine.
  • Wet food every evening. Toss any leftovers the next morning before giving a fresh portion. Wet food is in the box inside the cabinet.
💧
Water
  • He drinks from the white bowl. Refill with fresh water every other day.
  • He mostly ignores the fountain — don't worry about it.
🪴
Litter Box
  • Located in the office. Bags are right next to it.
  • Clean roughly 1–2 times per week, or as needed.
  • He loves going outside during the day and usually handles things out there.
🚪
Indoor / Outdoor
  • Leave the back door and side bedroom door open so he can wander freely during the day.
  • He usually comes back around 10pm — check the front door and side door. Just call his name if he's still out.
  • He has never stayed out all night. He always wants to come inside to sleep.
  • If you leave for a few hours while he's outside, no worries — just don't leave him out all night.
🧶
Personality
  • Very cuddly and chatty — he'll curl up on the couch, chair, or bed with you.
  • If he's feeling playful, he loves the rainbow long string toy.
  • Sleeps most of the day and wanders — completely normal.

The Garden

Try to get out there every other day when you can. If you miss a day, no stress at all. Priority is anything with fruit on it — tomatoes, avocados, and the peach tree.

🌀
Sprinklers — Every Other Day
  • Valves are on the side of the house — manual turn-on.
  • Closest to fence → front yard. Middle valve → backyard near back door. Farthest from gate → side yard by bedroom.
  • Run each about 5 minutes. Running longer is totally fine.
  • Move any cushions against the wall before turning them on.
🪣
Hand Watering — Every Other Day
  • The sprinklers don't reach everything, so a quick hand-water rounds it out.
  • About 30 seconds per spot — just give everything a good soak.
  • See the videos below for exactly which spots to hit.
🍅
Harvest — Help Yourself!
  • Cherry tomatoes and cucumbers are going crazy right now.
  • Blackberries and oranges are amazing.
  • Avocados are good once they ripen — squeeze gently to check.
  • Please eat whatever looks ripe. We mean it!
